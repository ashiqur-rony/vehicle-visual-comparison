Vehicle Comparison
A visual analysis tool to compare vehicle characteristics and make a purchase decision.
Ashiqur Rahman
Department of Computer Science
Northern Illinois University

The user can perform the following actions to explore the visualization:

  • Choosing any two numerical attributes for the vehicles will update the visualization axis.
  • Selecting a model under the filter will highlight that vehicle in the cluster.
  • Clicking on any of the bubble will filter the visualization based on the properties of the selected vehicle.
  • Removing the properties from filter will update the visualization by taking only the remaining properties.
